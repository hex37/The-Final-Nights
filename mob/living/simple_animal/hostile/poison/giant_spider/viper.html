Spider Viper
/mob/living/simple_animal/hostile/poison/giant_spider/viper

The assassin of spider subtypes. Essentially a juiced up version of the hunter.

A subtype of the giant spider which specializes in speed and poison. Injects a deadlier toxin than other spiders, moves extremely fast, but like the hunter has a limited amount of health.
